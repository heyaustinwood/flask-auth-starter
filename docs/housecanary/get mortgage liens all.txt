GET
Mortgage Liens - All
https://api.housecanary.com/v2/property/mortgage_lien_all?slug=123-Main-St-Apt-1-San-Francisco&address=123 Main St&unit=Apt 1&city=San Francisco&state=CA&zipcode=94105&order=asc&limit=&start=2015-01-01&end=2015-01-01
